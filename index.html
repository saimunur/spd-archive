SPD Representations Methods Archive

A public directory for the state-of-the-art symmetric positive definitive (SPD) representation methods publised or proposed in recent years and their results on the common fine-grained image benchmark datasets.

Introduction

Fine-grained image classification (FGIC) is one of the most challenging and active research area of computer vision. Recently, symmetric positive definitive (SPD) matrix-based visual representation methods have shown promising performance in FGIC. This page keeps track of the recent advances in SPD matrix-based visual representation methods for FGIC. Kindly refer to the contact section if you have any queries or sugesstions.
